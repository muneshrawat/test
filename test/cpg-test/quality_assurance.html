Quality Policy

"To maintain a leading position as a supplier and manufacturer of machinery (for cement, fertilizer, mineral processing, material handling equipment & general purpose), meeting the requirements of customer".

Quality at CPW has been at the core of all the business operations. Every product that rolls out of our production line bears the hallmarks of quality and excellence. This is achieved through a stringent quality control system backed up by an intensive research and development program. All our Quality Assurance Procedures are certified to be in compliance with ISO 9001:2008 standard and documented as per quality manual.

The Quality Assurance Department at CPW is a full fledged department, consisting of highly qualified and trained engineers. Quality Assurance Department is equipped with latest equipment to measure each and every quality parameter.

CPW Quality Engineers keep a vigilant eye on stage inspection by latest ERP solutions, which do not permit the production to go to the next stage of manufacturing before approval of existing stage by Quality Assurance Department.

Today, our products are built to standards that go beyond mere compliance to National and International norms and strive to benchmark improved levels in quality and perfection.

Safety Policy

At CPW, we are committed to the safety and well being of all employees and personnel who are in any way associated with our manufacturing operations. It is only through safe working practices and by creating safe working environments that a workforce can be encouraged and inspired to reach higher levels of productivity.

Our safety policy includes :

  • Consideration and understanding of safety and health in all aspects of our activities.
  • Assuring compliance with all relevant safety and health standards.
  • Ensuring safety through an interactive approach that empowers employees to demand better conditions.
  • A sustained and consistent improvement in safety standards.

Zero Tolerance Policy

At CPW, we have adopted a very stringent attitude to any irregularities in operations such as :

  • Constructing False Data.
  • Providing false statements or concealing essential truths intentionally.
  • Sabotage or trading of confidential information.
  • Partisanship towards suppliers for personal gain.
  • Compromise on quality.
  • Working against company's interests.
  • Theft or embezzlement.
  • Disorderly conduct of any sort.
  • Any employee caught indulging in any of the above activities shall be dealt with firmly.

Training and Orientation Programmes

As a leading player in the Indian Cement Turnkey industry we see growth as a prerequisite to sustained success. Its only through the enhancement of our capabilities in terms of equipment and manpower that we can stay the course of success. We regularly conduct training & orientation programmes that seek to educate our employees & empower them with new skills to meet the demanding challenges of the future.
