We offer turnkey solutions for lime plants including plant fabrication, installation & commissioning for Quick lime, Hydrated lime and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate plants. We offer lime plants including lime processing plant and lime processing machinery that incorporate the latest technology ensuing efficient functioning & optimum output.

Lime Plant

Features of CPW Lime and Dolo kilns

  • Lime kilns with oil fired, gas fired or dual fuel technology
  • Capacity currently upto 100 TPD. However we are also working on higher designs in collaboration
  • Effective pollution control devices for clean emission
  • Suitable for soft and uniform burning of lime and dolomite stone
  • Different grade stones can be processed for different applications
  • Low capital cost / ton production
  • High reliable and energy efficient plants
  • Fully automated kiln and material handling system
  • All material handling machinery can also be supplied
  • Gasifiers for clean coal gas fired kilns can also be supplied by us
